## Trial instructions

1. Create four top-level folders in a markdown vault: `1-Projects/`, `2-Areas/`, `3-Resources/`, `4-Archive/`, plus `0-Inbox/`.
2. For two weeks, capture everything into `0-Inbox/` without organizing.
3. Weekly: file inbox items into PARA; for anything worth keeping long-term, rewrite it as an atomic note in your own words and link it to at least two existing notes.
4. When ready to automate, encode steps 2–3 as instructions in a `CLAUDE.md` and let an agent do the weekly pass — this is the on-ramp to `../02-llm-wiki/`.
