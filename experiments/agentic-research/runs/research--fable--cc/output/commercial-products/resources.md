# Resources: Commercial Deep-Research Products

## Primary — vendor announcements and docs

- [Introducing deep research (OpenAI, Feb 2, 2025)](https://openai.com/index/introducing-deep-research/) — launch post: RL training claim, HLE 26.6%, GAIA SOTA, 5–30 min runtimes [vendor self-reported].
- [Deep research — OpenAI API guide](https://developers.openai.com/api/docs/guides/deep-research) — `o3-deep-research` / `o4-mini-deep-research` via Responses API; required data sources; MCP.
- [Try Deep Research… in Gemini (Google, Dec 11, 2024)](https://blog.google/products/gemini/google-gemini-deep-research/) — first "deep research" product launch; plan-approve-browse loop.
- [Gemini Deep Research overview](https://gemini.google/overview/deep-research/) — product page: how-it-works, Workspace grounding, report features.
- [Gemini Deep Research Agent — API docs](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/deep-research) — Interactions API models, background/streaming, $1–7 per-task estimates, 60-min limit.
- [Introducing Perplexity Deep Research (Feb 14, 2025)](https://www.perplexity.ai/hub/blog/introducing-perplexity-deep-research) — free-tier launch; HLE 21.1%, SimpleQA 93.9% [vendor self-reported].
- [Evaluating Deep Research Performance in the Wild with DRACO (Perplexity research, Jul 17, 2026)](https://research.perplexity.ai/articles/evaluating-deep-research-performance-in-the-wild-with-the-draco-benchmark) — open benchmark, 100 tasks, ~40 binary criteria each; Perplexity tops its own benchmark — conflict of interest flagged.
- [Anthropic Integrations + advanced Research announcement (X, May 2025)](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/1917972747000692919) — Research across web, Workspace, MCP Integrations.
- [How we built our multi-agent research system (Anthropic)](https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/multi-agent-research-system) — architecture behind the Claude Research product.
- [Claude Science (Anthropic, 2026)](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-science-ai-workbench) — research-workbench extension of the product line.
- [Introducing Deep Research in Azure AI Foundry Agent Service (Microsoft)](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/introducing-deep-research-in-azure-ai-foundry-agent-service/) — enterprise resale channel for OpenAI's agent.
