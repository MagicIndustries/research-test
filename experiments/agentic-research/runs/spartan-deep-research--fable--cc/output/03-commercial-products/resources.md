# Resources: Commercial Deep-Research Products

## OpenAI

- [Introducing deep research](https://openai.com/index/introducing-deep-research/) — 2025-02. Primary/vendor announcement (403 on direct fetch; numbers cross-checked via secondaries below). End-to-end RL training claim, HLE 26.6%, GAIA 67.36%.
- [Deep research API guide](https://developers.openai.com/api/docs/guides/deep-research) — current 2026. Primary. Models, required data-source tools, background mode, webhooks, clarify/rewrite prompting pattern, prompt-injection warning for MCP sources.
- [BrowseComp: a benchmark for browsing agents](https://openai.com/index/browsecomp/) — 2025-04. Primary/vendor. 1,266 hard-to-find-information questions; code in [openai/simple-evals](https://github.com/openai/simple-evals).
- [Fortune: deep research completes 26% of Humanity's Last Exam](https://fortune.com/2025/02/12/openai-deepresearch-humanity-last-exam/) — 2025-02-12. Secondary confirmation of launch-era claims.
- [TechRadar: Deep Research smashes records](https://www.techradar.com/computing/artificial-intelligence/openais-deep-research-smashes-records-for-the-worlds-hardest-ai-exam-with-chatgpt-o3-mini-and-deepseek-left-in-its-wake) — 2025-02. Secondary; notes the web-access asymmetry in HLE comparisons.
- [OpenRouter: o3-deep-research pricing](https://openrouter.ai/openai/o3-deep-research) / [o4-mini-deep-research](https://openrouter.ai/openai/o4-mini-deep-research) — secondary pricing listings.

## Google

- [Try Deep Research in Gemini](https://blog.google/products/gemini/google-gemini-deep-research/) — 2024-12-11. Primary/vendor launch post: plan/browse/report stages, editable plan, Gemini 1.5 Pro, Google Doc export.
- [Gemini Deep Research overview](https://gemini.google/overview/deep-research/) — current. Vendor marketing; 1M-token + RAG memory claim, async operation, self-critique passes.
- [Gemini API: Deep Research agent](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/interactions/deep-research) — current 2026. Primary developer docs (Interactions API).
- [Google Workspace blog: Meet Deep Research](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/meet-deep-research-your-new-ai-research-assistant) — vendor; Workspace rollout.
- [TechCrunch: Google launched its deepest research agent yet (same day as GPT-5.2)](https://techcrunch.com/2025/12/11/google-launched-its-deepest-ai-research-agent-yet-on-the-same-day-openai-dropped-gpt-5-2/) — 2025-12-11. Secondary; Gemini 3 Pro rebuild, DeepSearchQA open-sourcing, competitive benchmark claims (all vendor-run).

## Perplexity

- [Introducing Perplexity Deep Research](https://www.perplexity.ai/hub/blog/introducing-perplexity-deep-research) — 2025-02-14. Primary/vendor (403 on direct fetch). HLE 21.1%, SimpleQA 93.9%, 2–4 min runs.
- [TechCrunch: Perplexity launches freemium deep research](https://techcrunch.com/2025/02/15/perplexity-launches-its-own-freemium-deep-research-product/) — 2025-02-15. Secondary confirmation of launch and free tier.
- [DRACO benchmark article](https://research.perplexity.ai/articles/evaluating-deep-research-performance-in-the-wild-with-the-draco-benchmark) — 2026-07-17. Primary but vendor self-reporting throughout: tasks sampled from Perplexity's own production traffic; expert-revised binary rubrics (~45% revised); 3-judge reliability check; Perplexity first in all 10 domains. Methodologically interesting, competitively unpersuasive.
- [docs.perplexity.ai: sonar-deep-research](https://docs.perplexity.ai/docs/sonar/models/sonar-deep-research) — current. Primary/vendor API pricing: $2/$8 per M tokens + citation/reasoning/search-query fees, 128k context.

## Independent cost/quality context

- [FutureSearch: How much does deep research cost?](https://futuresearch.ai/blog/cost-of-deep-research/) — 2026-02-12 (upd. 2026-03-30). Primary independent measurement: $0.05–$0.55/task across 20+ configurations; Pareto framing.
- [DeepResearch Bench II](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08536) — 2026-01. Primary academic: strongest systems satisfy <50% of expert rubrics — calibration against vendor marketing.
