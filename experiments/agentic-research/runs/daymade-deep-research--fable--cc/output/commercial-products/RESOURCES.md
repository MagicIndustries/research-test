## Independent evaluation (read before trusting any vendor claim)

- **[19] Rao, Wong, Callison-Burch (UPenn) — Reference Hallucinations audit** — https://arxiv.org/html/2604.03173v1 — academic, 2026-04. ~168k URLs audited; Gemini DR 13.3% fabricated, OpenAI DR 3.5%, search-LLM baseline 4.8%; open-source `urlhealth` tool. The most important independent document on this approach.
- **[48] Sharma et al. (Scale AI) — ResearchRubrics** — https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.07685 — academic, 2025-11. Leading commercial agents under 68% expert-rubric compliance.
